About Us

Pathways for Hope and Action (PHA) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving food security, education, health, and livelihoods in Ethiopian communities. Our mission is to create hope through action, connecting resources with those in need and empowering communities to build a sustainable future.

Our Programs

Food Security

Providing sustainable food support and nutrition programs for vulnerable communities.

Education

Supporting schools, scholarships, and educational resources for children and adults.

Livelihoods & Community Development

Empowering communities through training, micro-enterprise support, and skill-building programs.
